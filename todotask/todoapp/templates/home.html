About

To-Do web app is a simple and user-friendly tool designed to help users stay organized and productive.
It allows users to add, edit, and delete tasks with ease, while also marking tasks as completed. The clean and
responsive design makes it accessible on both desktop and mobile devices. With clear icons and a smooth
interface, it’s perfect for students, professionals, or anyone looking to manage their daily tasks efficiently.
